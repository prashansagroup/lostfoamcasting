October 15, 2024

Lost Foam Casting Technology Achieves Limca Book of Records for World's Largest EPS Machine

Pune, India – Lost Foam Casting Technology Pvt. Ltd. (LFC Tech), a proud member of the Mane Group, is honored to announce its recognition by the **Limca Book of Records** for manufacturing the **World's Largest EPS Shape Moulding Machine**. This landmark achievement underscores LFC Tech's commitment to pushing the boundaries of foundry technology and pattern precision.

This monumental machinery allows the creation of extremely large, highly complex EPS patterns, revolutionizing the lost foam casting process for heavy-duty industrial applications, including engine blocks and large machine casings. This record is a testament to the in-house engineering expertise and innovative spirit driving the "New Era of Foundry."
